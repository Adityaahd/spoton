About SpotOn

SpotOn is a music recommendation platform that helps you discover new songs based on your Spotify listening history. Our goal is to provide you with a unique and engaging experience, combining the best song recommendation system with interactive features and a visually appealing design.

Created by music enthusiasts, SpotOn aims to bring you closer to the music you love and introduce you to new favorites.
